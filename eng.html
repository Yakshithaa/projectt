CGPA refers to the Cumulative Grade Point Average which literally translates to the sum of all your current credit points.This system helps in accessing the overall academic performance of the student.



SGPA, which stands for Semester Grade Point Average is an evaluation method that highlights the semester wise performance of the student.It can be calculated by simply adding all the credit points awarded for the subjects and then dividing it by the total credits alloted to that semester
